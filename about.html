About

Faraba connects consumers, retailers, and businesses directly to Ghanaian farmers, providing a transparent and convenient platform for fresh, local produce with fair pricing. Farmers benefit from a reliable market and reduced post-harvest losses.

Product Roadmap

Our collaboration with Tricode for the development of the FarabaMarket mobile application follows a well-defined product roadmap. This roadmap ensures we deliver a high-quality and user-friendly app that meets the needs of our users.

Stage 1: Market Research

We are currently conducting extensive market research to understand the needs and preferences of our target audience. This involves analyzing market trends, customer behavior, and competitor offerings to ensure our app provides the most relevant features and services.

Stage 2: Data Survey

In this phase, we are collecting and analyzing data from potential users through surveys and feedback sessions. This data will help us tailor the app's features to meet user expectations and enhance their overall experience.

Upcoming Stages

The next stages in our product development roadmap include:

  • Design and Prototyping: Creating detailed designs and prototypes based on the insights gathered from market research and data surveys.
  • Development and Testing: Developing the app with a focus on quality and performance, followed by rigorous testing to ensure a seamless user experience.
  • Launch and Feedback: Launching the app and continuously collecting user feedback to make necessary improvements and updates.
